Obesity 

PREVENTION, SYMPTOMS, TREATMENT

Obesity

Obesity is a disorder involving excessive body fat that increases the risk of health problems. It is caused when more calories are taken in than are burned by exercise and daily activities.
Obesity is usually the result of an unbalanced diet and bad lifestyle. 


Childhood Obesity

This condition is when excess body fat negatively affects the health and well being of a child.
There are a range of factors which can result in childhood obesity.
Few of these include overeating, eating unhealthy food regularly, environment, little to no physical activities, genetics etc. 

SYMPTOMS

  • Excess body fat
  • Shortness of breath 
  • Sweating more than usual
  • Trouble sleeping
  • Joint pain, low back pain or Backaches

Image Description

TREATMENT

  • Taking self care.
  • Maintaining balanced diet and good exercise
  • Visiting bariatricians in case of severe obesity
  • Intensive behavioral therapy is also a good solution for obesity, in it you learn how to change eating and exercise habits 

PREVENTIONS FOR CHILDHOOD OBESITY

  • Substitute higher nutrient, low calorie foods such as fruits and vegetables, in place of  foods with added sugar and solid fats, encourage homemade and healthy snacks.
  • Take part in regular physical activity. 
  • Make sure children get adequate sleep ( 6 to 8 hours).
  • Limiting unhealthy food and reducing screen time can help
  • New mobile technologies like wearable, deployable etc. are providing unprecedented opportunities for innovation in obesity prevention and treatment.
  • Eat plenty of dietary fibers.
  • Focus on eating food with low- glycemic index 
  • There could be more physical education periods and sports programs in school.
